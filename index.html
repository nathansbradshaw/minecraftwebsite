About The Mod

The pokemans

Pikachu - spawns in most biomes. Can be bred with sweet berries.
Evee - spawns in most biomes. Can be bred with sweet berries.
Clefairy - spawns in most biomes. Can be bred with sweet berries.

Evolve Them!

Use the correct Elemental stone to evolve your pokemon
Ther are several different types of stones
Find Shinys too!

New Crafting Recipes!

Redstone bar used to craft the poke ball. Items needed: 1x Iron, 4x Redstone
Pokeball - use this on a pokemon to capture them. Items needed: 3x Redston Bar, 2x Iron nugget, 1x Ender pearl, 3x Iron Bar.
Shiny Charm - Use this on a normal pokemon to turn them shiny! Items needed: 4x Lapis, 1x Nether Star
Fire Stone - use this on a fire type pokemon to evolve it. Items needed: 8x Blaze Powder, 1x Enchantment Bottle
Water Stone - use this on a water type pokemon to evolve it. Items needed: 8x Lapiz, 1x Enchantment Bottle
Thunder Stone - use this on a thunder type pokemon to evolve it. Items needed: 8x Emeralds, 1x Enchantment Bottle
Moon Stone - use this on a moon type pokemon to evolve it. Items needed: 8x Gunpowder, 1x Iron Bar
Rare Candy - use this on evee to change it to Espeon (used during the day), Umbreon (used at night).
